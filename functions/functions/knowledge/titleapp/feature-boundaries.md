# TitleApp Feature Boundaries

## What TitleApp DOES
- Document management and digital certificates
- Workflow automation for professional services
- AI Digital Workers for specific industry verticals (Real Estate, Aviation, Auto Dealer, Government, Web3, Solar, Title & Escrow, Platform)
- Secure Vault for business documents with version control
- Document Control with acknowledgment tracking and blockchain audit trail
- CoPilot mode for aviation (flight ops, currency tracking, training)
- Sandbox for building and testing custom Digital Workers
- Marketing and content creation tools (drafts, social posting, email campaigns)
- Contact management and CRM-lite features
- Usage metering with inference credits

## What TitleApp DOES NOT Do
- Banking, payment processing, or money transmission
- Legal advice, tax preparation, or accounting certifications
- Medical diagnosis, treatment recommendations, or clinical decisions
- Replace human judgment in high-stakes decisions
- Guarantee regulatory compliance (we provide tools, not legal opinions)
- Offer investment advice or securities recommendations
- Process insurance claims or underwriting
