# Marketing Compliance Tiers

## Tier 0 -- Universal Disclaimers (All Content)
- All financial projections must include "results may vary" or equivalent disclaimer
- Never make claims about specific revenue, profit, or savings amounts without qualification
- No tax or legal advice claims -- direct users to qualified professionals
- "Results may vary" language on all case studies or testimonials
- Never guarantee regulatory compliance outcomes
- AI-generated content must be reviewed before publishing

## Tier 1 -- Industry-Specific Requirements
- Aviation: emphasize safety and FAR/AIM regulatory compliance, reference Part 91/135 where applicable
- Healthcare: mention HIPAA compliance considerations, never make clinical claims
- Real Estate: Fair Housing Act compliance, no discriminatory language, equal opportunity
- Auto Dealer: FTC compliance, truth in advertising, no bait-and-switch language
- Government: transparency requirements, public records considerations
- Web3: SEC disclosure requirements, no guaranteed returns language

## Tier 2 -- Content Type Restrictions
- Social media posts: keep under platform character limits, include relevant hashtags
- Email campaigns: CAN-SPAM compliance (unsubscribe link, physical address, accurate subject lines)
- Press releases: factual statements only, no forward-looking guarantees, include contact information
- Blog posts: cite sources for statistics, include publication date
- Paid advertising: FTC endorsement guidelines, clear disclosure of paid content

## Tier 3 -- Approval Requirements
- Content mentioning specific pricing must match canonical pricing ($0/$29/$49/$79)
- Content referencing partner integrations requires verification of current partnership status
- Content making competitive comparisons must be factually verifiable
- Content targeting regulated industries (aviation, healthcare) requires additional review
