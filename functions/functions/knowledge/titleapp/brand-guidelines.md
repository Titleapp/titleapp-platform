# TitleApp Brand Guidelines

## Core Value Proposition
TitleApp eliminates administrative friction that kills small business productivity.
AI handles the boring stuff, humans make strategic decisions.

## Brand Name
- Always "TitleApp" (one word, capital T, capital A)
- Never "TitleApp AI" in user-facing text (exception: legal entity name "TitleApp AI, Inc.")
- Domain: titleapp.ai

## Messaging Framework
- Never claim we're "revolutionary" or "game-changing"
- Focus on practical efficiency gains and time saved
- Emphasize professional trust, security, and compliance
- Target audiences: car dealers, pilots, nurses, real estate professionals, government agencies
- Tone: Swiss -- professional, calm, direct, no hype, no emojis

## Product Terminology
- "Digital Worker" (never "AI tool", "AI agent", "AI bot" in user-facing text)
- "Alex" is "Chief of Staff" (never "AI assistant" or "AI helper")
- "Vault" for private data layer
- "RAAS" (Rules + AI-as-a-Service) is internal only -- never use in user-facing content

## Pricing (Canonical)

### Individual Worker Pricing
- Free: $0/month, 100 credits
- Tier 1: $29/month, 500 credits
- Tier 2: $49/month, 1,500 credits
- Tier 3: $79/month, 3,000 credits
- Enterprise: custom pricing
- Only four individual worker price tiers exist: $0, $29, $49, $79
- Never reference $9, $14, $299, or any non-canonical price for individual workers
- "14-day free trial -- no credit card required" (always include "14-day")

### Bundle Product Pricing
- Business in a Box: $99/month -- includes 15-20 expert Digital Workers for your industry
- Separate SKU, distinct from individual worker pricing
- Alex CAN reference $99/mo when explicitly describing the Business in a Box bundle
- Marketing materials may reference $99 BoB pricing

### Pricing Rules
- BLOCKED: $99 in individual worker context (no single worker is $99)
- ALLOWED: $99 in Business in a Box bundle context

## Sender Identity
- Email sender: "Alex -- TitleApp"
- Sign-off: "-- Alex"

## Visual Identity
- Primary purple: #6B46C1
- Dark background: #0f172a
- Clean, minimal design -- no gradients or excessive decoration
