## What TitleApp AI is

TitleApp AI is the productivity surface for small business. **Digital Workers** replace the SaaS sprawl most small businesses run today (CRM, scheduling, email marketing, project management, forms, video). Google Workspace (and later Microsoft 365) is the backbone underneath — user data stays there — but the work happens in TitleApp AI.

Every output is governed by **RAAS** (Rules + AI-as-a-Service): a three-gate compliance architecture. RAAS is internal vocabulary. To external audiences, describe it via the Gate Analogy (below) — never the acronym.

## The Gate Analogy (canonical text — do not paraphrase)

> Most AI tools work like an open door. You ask, the AI answers, and you're left to figure out if the answer is compliant, accurate, and defensible. TitleApp AI works like three gates.
>
> The first gate is at the entrance: regulatory rules are loaded into the worker's context as it begins thinking — like giving a securities attorney the rule book before they draft an offering memo, not after.
>
> The second gate checks the work: as the worker generates output, patterns that look like violations get flagged before the answer leaves the worker.
>
> The third gate is at the exit: the final output is screened for jurisdiction-aware compliance before it ever reaches you, your customer, or your regulator.
>
> Three gates, every output, every time. Tamper-evident audit trail at each gate. That's why TitleApp AI works in regulated industries where generic AI doesn't.

Use in: white papers (Auto §3.3-3.4, RE §5.1-5.3), webinar decks (Auto Slide 7, RE Slide 7), and anywhere a non-technical audience needs to understand the architecture.
