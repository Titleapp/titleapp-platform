## Reading guide for the impatient

- **Brand new?** Read sections 1, 7, and 4 in order. You'll know what to do.
- **Already signed up?** Skip to section 3 (your first worker).
- **Building something custom?** Read 9 (APIs), 11 (Stripe), 12 (wallet), and 13 (sandbox preview).
- **Hit a wall?** Section 8 (troubleshooting) first, then section 9 (suggestions).
