**Last confirmed: Session 28 (March 2026)**

## Subscription Tiers

| Tier | Price | Credits | Use Case |
|------|-------|---------|----------|
| Free | $0/mo | 100 | Vault + basic workers |
| Tier 1 | $29/mo | 500 | Standard workers + Pilot Pro |
| Tier 2 | $49/mo | 1,500 | Regulated workers + Pilot Pro+ |
| Tier 3 | $79/mo | 3,000 | Premium/regulated workers |

- 14-day free trial (no credit card)

## Alex (Chief of Staff)
Free with 3+ worker subscriptions

- CFI/CFII academy instructors: Free Pilot Pro+ while on staff (CFIACADEMY coupon)
- CFI departure grace: CFIPRO → 3 months free → $29/mo
