To:  Vendors, suppliers, service providers, contract counterparties, lenders,

NOTICE IS HEREBY GIVEN that, pursuant to action duly taken by the Managing
Members of the Company and in accordance with Sections 18-803 and 18-804 of the
Delaware Limited Liability Company Act (6 Del. C. § 18-101 et seq.):

  1. The Company has been dissolved effective May [__], 2026 and is now in
     the process of winding up its business and affairs.

  2. The Company will pay or otherwise make reasonable provision for all
     known and reasonably ascertainable claims and obligations of the Company
     before distributing any remaining assets.

  3. ALL CLAIMS AGAINST THE COMPANY MUST BE PRESENTED IN WRITING to the
     address set forth below NO LATER THAN [Date — 120 days from the date
     of this notice, but in any event a date that is at least 120 days
     following actual receipt of this notice]. Any claim presented after
     that date may be barred under 6 Del. C. § 18-804.

  4. Each claim must contain the following information so that the Company
     can evaluate it:

        (a)  the name, mailing address, telephone number, and email address
             of the claimant;
        (b)  a clear statement of the basis of the claim, including the
             contract, invoice, purchase order, agreement, or other source
             giving rise to the claim;
        (c)  the date(s) on which the obligation arose;
        (d)  the amount claimed, broken out into principal, interest, fees,
             and costs as applicable;
        (e)  copies of any supporting documentation (invoices, statements,
             agreements, correspondence, etc.); and
        (f)  the claimant's signature.

     the claimant commences an appropriate proceeding within 90 days of the
     date of rejection, in accordance with 6 Del. C. § 18-804.

  7. This notice is being given to known claimants by direct delivery (mail
     or email) and is being published more broadly to provide notice to any
     unknown claimants.
