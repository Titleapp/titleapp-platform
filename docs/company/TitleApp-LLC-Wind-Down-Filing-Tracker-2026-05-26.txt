these dates:

  A. Tell Accounting worker in chat: "Please track these LLC wind-down

     deadline surface for you.
  B. Add them yourself in the Accounting Actions panel.
  C. Have me load them directly via Firestore script when you confirm
     dates (I need final dissolution-effective date before I can do this
     cleanly).

I recommend (A) — exercises the worker, and the loop is what catches
schedule slippage. Sean lost $20K in prior filing penalties trusting
himself; the rule is "every deadline-driven workflow MUST surface
