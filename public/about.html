About TitleApp

TitleApp helps you make sense of a messy world. We provide a secure and immutable record of any asset's title records, chain of title, and transfer.

Our AI- and blockchain-native software simplifies record keeping, improves security and accuracy, increases transparency, empowers communities, and saves time and money.

Our Vision

In a post-screen world where AI is embedded into our lives, we provide the unbreakable source of truth.

We use AI to make managing important assets effortless while anchoring them on blockchain to guarantee immutability and transparency.
