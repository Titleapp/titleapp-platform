## Sending strategy for the 3,000-connection list

### Phase 1 — Hand-curate the top 100 (today + tomorrow)

You and Kent each pick the **top 50 connections** — people you'd actually call. Send personalized messages with the relationship-hook line. These are the ones who actually register and bring others.

### Phase 2 — Standard message to the rest (next week)

The remaining ~2,900: standard version, sent in batches of 100/day per account. Use the standard template.

### Phase 3 — Re-engagement after the webinars (June)

People who registered but didn't attend → replay link + white paper download. People who attended → "Become a Certified Creator" follow-up.

---

## LinkedIn-specific rules

- **Don't paste the link in the first message.** LinkedIn deprioritizes external links. Either send the link in a follow-up after they reply, OR include it but accept slightly lower delivery.
- **Skip "Hope you're doing well"** — overused.
- **Each account: max 100 messages/day.** Two accounts (Sean + Kent) = 200/day.
- **No images, no GIFs in the first message.**
- **Sign off as you'd sign a personal note.** Just `— Sean` or `— Kent`.
