# RaaS — Real Estate Contracts — Nevada (NV)

This directory contains **baseline contract templates** approved for use within
Nevada real estate transactions under the TitleApp RaaS framework.

All contracts are:
- Conservative by default
- Statute-aligned
- Client-modifiable **only at allowed fields**

---

## Contract Philosophy

TitleApp provides **working legal primitives**, not bespoke legal advice.

These contracts:
- Reduce operational friction
- Prevent missing-required-language failures
- Standardize downstream automation (escrow, title, reporting)

Clients remain legally responsible for:
- Final review
- Execution
- Jurisdictional applicability

---

## Included Contract Types (Baseline)

### Sales
- Residential Purchase Agreement (Baseline)
- Commercial Purchase Agreement (Baseline)

### Leasing
- Residential Lease Agreement
- Commercial Lease (Shell Only)

### Financing (High-Level)
- Seller Financing Addendum
- Lease-to-Own Addendum

---

## Prohibited Actions

Clients MAY NOT:
- Remove statutory disclosures referenced by these contracts
- Disable mandatory contingency language
- Alter consumer-protection clauses

---

## Contract Customization Rules

Allowed:
- Purchase price
- Dates & timelines
- Property description
- Optional contingencies

Restricted:
- Disclosure language
- Statutory notices
- Termination rights required by law

---

## Versioning

Each contract template MUST include:
- Version number
- Effective date
- Governing jurisdiction (NV)
- Statutory references (where applicable)
