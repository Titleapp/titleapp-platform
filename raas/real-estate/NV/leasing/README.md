# RAAS — Real Estate Leasing (Nevada)

## Purpose
This directory defines the **Rules as a Service (RAAS)** for residential and commercial leasing
transactions in the State of Nevada.

These rules ensure that TitleApp supports leasing activity in a compliant, predictable,
and auditable manner without requiring clients to supply their own legal frameworks.

---

## Covered Lease Types
- Residential lease
- Multi-family residential lease
- Commercial lease
- Month-to-month tenancy
- Lease renewals and extensions
- Lease amendments
- Early lease termination
- Property management leasing activity

---

## Core RAAS Responsibilities
- Enforce Nevada-specific landlord–tenant rules
- Surface required lease disclosures
- Guide compliant leasing workflows
- Prevent unsupported or non-compliant lease actions
- Maintain auditability of lease-related activity

---

## Regulatory References (Informational)
- Nevada Revised Statutes (NRS) Chapter 118A — Landlord and Tenant
- Nevada Real Estate Division
- Local municipal leasing ordinances (where applicable)

> ⚠️ These references are informational only.  
> TitleApp does not provide legal advice.

---

## Client Overrides
Clients may add supplemental lease terms or documents.
All client-supplied content is logged and does not override mandatory RAAS safeguards.
