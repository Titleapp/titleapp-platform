
# RAAS — Real Estate Leasing (CA)

## Purpose
This directory defines the **Rules as a Service (RAAS)** for residential and commercial leasing
transactions in California.

These rules govern how TitleApp supports leasing workflows out of the box.

---

## Covered Lease Types
- Residential lease
- Multi-family lease
- Commercial lease
- Month-to-month tenancy
- Lease renewals and extensions
- Lease amendments
- Early termination scenarios

---

## Core RAAS Responsibilities
- Ensure required disclosures are surfaced
- Enforce jurisdiction-specific lease rules
- Guide compliant leasing workflows
- Prevent unsupported or non-compliant actions

---

## Regulatory Sources (Reference Only)
- California Civil Code (Landlord/Tenant)
- California Department of Real Estate (DRE)
- Local rent control ordinances (city-level)

> ⚠️ These references are informational.  
> TitleApp does not provide legal advice.
