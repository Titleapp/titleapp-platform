# California Real Estate Licensing
# Path: raas/real-estate/CA/licensing.md
# Source: California Department of Real Estate (DRE)

---

## License Types

### Salesperson
- 3 college-level courses (Real Estate Principles, Real Estate Practice, one elective)
- State exam (salesperson exam)
- Must work under a licensed broker
- Renewal: every 4 years
- First renewal: 45 hours CE
- Subsequent renewals: 45 hours CE (includes ethics, agency, fair housing, trust fund, risk management)

### Broker
- 2 years active as salesperson (within last 5 years)
- 8 college-level courses
- State exam (broker exam â€” harder than salesperson)
- Can operate independently, employ salespersons

### Property Management
- Requires a real estate license (broker or salesperson under broker)
- No separate PM license in California
- Exception: resident manager of 16+ units can be unlicensed

## DRE Advertising Rules
- Must include: broker's name (as licensed with DRE) on all advertising
- License number: DRE requires license number on all "first point of contact" materials (advertising, business cards, websites, email signatures)
- Team names: must include brokerage name
- "CalDRE#" or "DRE#" preceding the number
- AI ensures all marketing includes broker name and DRE license number
