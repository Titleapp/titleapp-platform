## Overview

Real estate is one of the most heavily regulated industries in the United States. The AI must be aware of and comply with federal, state, and local regulations. This folder covers federal compliance. State-specific compliance is in each jurisdiction folder.

## Key Federal Laws

1. **Fair Housing Act** — see `fair-housing.md`
2. **RESPA** — see `respa.md`
3. **ADA** — see `ada.md`
4. **Environmental** — see `environmental.md`
5. **FCRA** (Fair Credit Reporting Act) — tenant screening
6. **TCPA** — marketing communications (see global marketing rules)
7. **CAN-SPAM** — email marketing (see global marketing rules)
8. **ECOA** (Equal Credit Opportunity Act) — lending discrimination
9. **Truth in Lending (TILA)** — mortgage disclosures (see mortgage vertical)

## AI Compliance Behavior

### Before ANY external communication:
- Check Fair Housing compliance
- Check TCPA/CAN-SPAM compliance
- Check state-specific advertising rules
- Check license display requirements

### Before ANY tenant screening:
- Ensure consistent criteria applied to all applicants
- FCRA-compliant adverse action notices for denials
- Document screening criteria and results

### Before ANY referral or compensation:
- Check RESPA for prohibited kickbacks
- Disclose all referral relationships
- Document all compensation arrangements

### When in doubt:
- Flag for human review
- NEVER give legal advice
- Recommend the client consult an attorney
- Document the issue and the recommendation
