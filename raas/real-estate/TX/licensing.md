# Texas Real Estate Licensing
# Path: raas/real-estate/TX/licensing.md
# Source: Texas Real Estate Commission (TREC)

---

## License Types

### Sales Agent
- 180 hours pre-license education (6 courses)
- State exam
- Must be sponsored by a licensed broker
- Renewal: every 2 years
- First renewal: additional 98 hours of education (SAE)
- Subsequent renewals: 18 hours CE (includes Legal Update I and II)

### Broker
- 4 years active experience as a sales agent (within last 5 years)
- 630 total education hours
- State exam
- Can operate independently, sponsor agents

## TREC Advertising Rules
- Must include: broker's name (as registered with TREC) prominently displayed
- Team names: must include brokerage name
- Agent name: must be identical to name on TREC license
- License number: not required but recommended
- "Equal Housing Opportunity" statement on all advertising
- AI ensures all marketing materials include required brokerage identification

## Property Management Licensing
- Texas requires a real estate license to manage property for others for compensation
- Must be a licensed broker OR work under a broker
- Exception: on-site manager of a single property
- TREC has specific rules about property management trust accounts
