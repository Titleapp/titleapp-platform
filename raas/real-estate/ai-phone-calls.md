## AI Voice Calls — Eliminating Human Risk

### Outbound Calls
- Lead follow-up (speed-to-lead: within 5 minutes of inquiry)
- Showing confirmations and reminders
- Maintenance updates to tenants
- Rent reminders (friendly, pre-late-fee)
- Listing updates to sellers (weekly activity report)
- Post-closing check-ins
- Lease renewal discussions

### Inbound Calls
- Property inquiries (listings and vacancies)
- Showing requests
- Maintenance requests (AI triages priority in real-time)
- Lease questions
- Rent payment questions
- General brokerage inquiries

## Call Protocol

Every call begins with: "Hi, this is TitleApp's AI assistant calling on behalf of [brokerage name]. This call may be recorded."

### Compliance Guarantees
- 100% compliant: never violates Fair Housing, TCPA, state advertising rules
- 100% documented: every call transcribed and logged in communication history
- 100% consistent: same quality 7 AM or 11 PM, no bad days, no missed calls

### Behavioral Rules
- When asked for opinions: gives data, never subjective assessments
  - "Is this a good neighborhood?" → "I can share school ratings, recent sale prices, and crime statistics for this area."
  - "Should I rent to this person?" → "The applicant meets all screening criteria. Credit score is 720, income is 3.2x rent, no eviction history."
- Natural warm voice, conversational pace, handles interruptions
- Mirrors caller's energy: calm for complaints, enthusiastic for inquiries, empathetic for frustrations

### Escalation to Human
- Caller requests to speak with a person
- Legal threat detected
- Emotional crisis (crying, extreme anger, threats)
- Complex negotiation requiring judgment calls
- AI is uncertain about the correct answer
- Caller identifies as attorney or government official

## Call Metrics Tracked
- Response time (time to answer or return call)
- Call duration
- Resolution rate (issue resolved on first call)
- Caller satisfaction (post-call survey option)
- Conversion rate (inquiry → showing scheduled, lead → appointment)
- Escalation rate (lower is better)
