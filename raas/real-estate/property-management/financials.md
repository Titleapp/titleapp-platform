## Revenue Tracking

### Rent Collection
- Track: due, paid, late, partial, delinquent, written off
- Calculate: collection rate = collected / billed
- Late fees assessed automatically per lease terms
- NSF fees for returned payments
- Partial payment policy: accept or reject? (jurisdiction-specific â€” some states require acceptance)

### Fee Income
- Management fee: % of collected rent (calculated monthly)
- Leasing fee: flat amount or % of first month's rent
- Lease renewal fee: flat amount
- Maintenance markup: % added to vendor invoices
- Application fee: per applicant (must not exceed actual cost in some states)
- Move-out fee / cleaning fee (if applicable and disclosed in lease)
- Late fee income (split with owner per agreement)

## Expense Tracking

### Property Expenses (paid from owner's account)
- Maintenance and repairs
- Property taxes
- Insurance premiums
- HOA fees
- Utilities (if owner-paid)
- Landscaping and pool service
- Pest control
- Legal fees (evictions, disputes)
- Advertising/marketing (vacancy)
- Capital improvements

### Management Company Expenses
- Office overhead
- Software and technology
- Insurance (E&O, general liability)
- Marketing (company-level)
- Payroll

## Owner Statements

### Monthly Statement Contents
- Beginning balance
- Income: rent collected, other income (late fees, utility reimbursements)
- Expenses: itemized by category with vendor name and description
- Management fee
- Net disbursement to owner
- Ending balance (reserve, if applicable)
- Vacancy status
- Maintenance summary
- Upcoming items (renewals, inspections, planned maintenance)

### Annual Reporting
- 1099-MISC for rental income (if over $600)
- Year-end P&L by property
- Capital expenditure summary
- Occupancy rate history
- Maintenance cost analysis
- Rent growth analysis
- Market comparison: "Your property earned $X this year. Similar properties in the area averaged $Y."

## Reserve Account
- Some management agreements require an owner reserve (typically $500-$2,000)
- Reserve covers emergency repairs without waiting for owner approval
- AI tracks reserve balance and alerts when below minimum
- Replenished from rent proceeds monthly

## Trust Account Compliance
- ALL tenant deposits and owner funds held in trust/escrow account
- NEVER commingled with management company operating funds
- Per state law: FL requires separate accounts, interest-bearing for deposits. TX requires separate accounts. CA requires trust accounts.
- AI tracks all trust account transactions for compliance
