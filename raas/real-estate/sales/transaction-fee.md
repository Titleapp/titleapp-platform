# Transaction Fee
# Path: raas/real-estate/sales/transaction-fee.md

---

## TitleApp Platform Fee: 1.0% of Closed Transaction Value

- Charged only on closed deals (if deal falls through, no charge)
- Deducted from commission disbursement at closing
- Disclosed at onboarding (Phase 0) and at every transaction initiation
- AI confirms: "This transaction at $485,000 will include the TitleApp platform fee of 1% ($4,850) at closing. This covers my management of the entire transaction."

## Applicability

**Applies to:**
- Residential sales (buy and sell side)
- Commercial sales

**Does NOT apply to:**
- Lease transactions
- STR bookings

## Property Management Revenue Model

Property management revenue comes from:
- Subscription + usage fees
- Rent processing margin
- $200-500 leasing fee per new tenant placed
