## Systems Overview

### Electrical
- Dual alternators (28V system)
- Dual batteries
- Essential bus for critical instruments

### Fuel
- Two wing tanks (left/right)
- Fuel selector: LEFT / RIGHT
- Electric fuel pump with PRIME / BOOST / OFF positions
- Engine-driven fuel pump (primary)

### Flight Controls
- Conventional stick and rudder
- Electric pitch trim
- Manual flap lever: UP (0%) / 50% / 100%

### CAPS (Cirrus Airframe Parachute System)
- Whole-aircraft ballistic recovery parachute
- Max deployment speed (Vpd): 135 KIAS
- Activation: Pull T-handle on cabin ceiling
- Rocket-deployed parachute extracts aircraft to survivable descent rate

## Balked Landing / Go-Around

- Best angle of climb speed: 81-83 KIAS (flaps 50%)
- Apply full power, retract flaps to 50%, climb at 81-83 KIAS
- At safe altitude, retract flaps to UP, accelerate to Vy

## Disclaimer

This reference file is a structured summary of data extracted from the Cirrus SR20 Information Manual (June 2007 edition). Pilots must always refer to the FAA-approved Airplane Flight Manual specific to their aircraft serial number for regulatory compliance and operational decisions. Performance data is approximate and based on standard conditions unless noted. Actual performance will vary with aircraft condition, configuration, weight, altitude, temperature, and pilot technique.
