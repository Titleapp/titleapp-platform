### Pressurization
- 200: 6.0 psid max differential
- B200: 6.5 psid max differential
- B200GT: 6.6 psid max differential
- Automatic and manual controller
- Safety valve prevents exceeding max differential
- Negative pressure relief valve

### Flight Controls
- Conventional pushrod-and-cable operated
- T-tail configuration
- Electrically operated flaps and trim
- Dual push rod actuators

### Ice Protection
- Engine inlet anti-ice (bleed air)
- Propeller anti-ice (electric)
- Wing and empennage deice boots
- Windshield anti-ice (max 226 KIAS)
- Min speed in icing: 140 KIAS

## 10. Single-Engine Procedures

### Critical Speeds (OEI)
| Speed | Value | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Vmca | 86 KIAS | Min control speed, air |
| Vyse (blue line) | 121 KIAS | Best rate of climb, OEI |
| Vxse | 115 KIAS | Best angle of climb, OEI |
| Emergency Descent | 181 KIAS | |
| Max Range Glide | 135 KIAS | |

### Engine Failure After Takeoff (Memory Items)
1. Mixtures -- FULL FORWARD
2. Props -- FULL FORWARD
3. Power levers -- MAX ALLOWABLE
4. Flaps -- UP
5. Landing gear -- UP (verify positive rate of climb)
6. Identify -- dead foot = dead engine
7. Verify -- retard power lever on failed engine
8. Failed engine -- SECURE (fuel condition lever CUTOFF, prop feather, generator OFF)
9. Accelerate to Vyse (121 KIAS) or above

### Single-Engine Approach (B200GT)
- Double the torque setting used for the normal approach on the operating engine
- Rudder boost activates at approximately 1,400 ft-lbs torque
- Maintain Vyse or Vref+10 (whichever is higher) until landing is assured
- Go-around: apply max allowable power on operating engine, retract flaps and gear

### Engine Securing Procedure
1. Power lever -- IDLE
2. Fuel condition lever -- CUTOFF
3. Prop lever -- FEATHER
4. Generator switch -- OFF
5. Bleed air -- CLOSED (failed engine side)
6. Firewall shutoff -- AS REQUIRED

### Notes
- Maintain Vyse (blue line, 121 KIAS) until at safe altitude
- Bank 3-5 degrees toward operating engine for zero sideslip
- Do not attempt restart below safe altitude unless cause identified and corrected
- Refer to POH Emergency Procedures for complete checklists
