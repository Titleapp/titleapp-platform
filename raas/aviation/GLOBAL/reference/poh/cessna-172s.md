## Operating Limitations

### Normal Category
Approved maneuvers: Chandelles (entry max 105 KIAS), Lazy Eights (entry max 105 KIAS), Steep Turns (entry max 95 KIAS). No aerobatic maneuvers including spins are approved.

### Utility Category
All normal category maneuvers plus spins (slow deceleration from straight and level, 1-turn spin, immediate recovery). Must be within utility category weight and CG limits. Only front seats occupied, no baggage.

### General Limitations
- Flight into known icing conditions: Prohibited
- Maximum operating altitude: 14,000 ft (service ceiling)
- Maximum demonstrated crosswind component: 15 knots
- Aerobatic flight (normal category): Prohibited
- Intentional spinning (normal category): Prohibited

## Systems Summary

### Electrical
- 28-volt DC system
- 60-amp belt-driven alternator
- 24-volt battery
- Dual bus architecture (essential bus and avionics bus) with G1000

### Pitot / Static
- Heated pitot tube (left wing)
- Dual static ports (fuselage sides)
- Alternate static source (cabin)

### Avionics
- Garmin G1000 NXi integrated flight deck
- Dual AHRS, dual ADC
- GFC 700 Automatic Flight Control System (autopilot)
- Built-in GPS (WAAS), COM/NAV, transponder, audio panel

### Flight Controls
- Conventional cable-actuated ailerons, elevator, rudder
- Manually operated flaps (0, 10, 20, 30 degrees)
- Adjustable stabilator trim (electric)
