# Pilatus PC-12 NGX -- Pilot's Information Manual Reference

Document: PC-12 NGX PIM, MSN 1720/2001 and up, Issue 003, Rev 03
Aircraft Model: PC-12/47E (NGX variant)
EASA Type Certificate: EASA.A.123

---

## Section 1 -- General

### Aircraft Description

Single-engine turboprop, pressurized, retractable tricycle landing gear.
Manufacturer: Pilatus Aircraft Ltd., Stans, Switzerland.

### Engine

- Type: Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6E-67XP
- Configuration: Free turbine, reverse-flow annular combustion chamber
- FADEC: Full Authority Digital Engine Control (dual-channel)
- Maximum Shaft Horsepower: 1,200 SHP (takeoff and max climb)
- Maximum Cruise SHP: 1,100 SHP
- Ng Speed at 104%: 38,967 rpm
- Np (Propeller Speed): 1,700 rpm
- Propeller Low Speed: 1,550 rpm

### Propeller

- Type: Hartzell HC-E5A-31A/NC10245B
- Blades: 5-blade, constant speed, full feathering, reversible
- Diameter: 104-105 in (2.642-2.667 m)
- Fine Pitch: 14.7 deg
- Reverse Pitch: -17.5 deg
- Feather Pitch: 80.0 deg
- Minimum In-Flight Pitch: 1 deg

### Dimensions

- Wingspan: 53 ft 6 in (16.31 m)
- Length: 47 ft 3 in (14.40 m)
- Height: 14 ft 0 in (4.26 m)
- Wing Area: 277.8 sq ft (25.81 sq m)

### Cabin Dimensions

- Width: 5 ft 0 in (1.52 m)
- Floor Width: 4 ft 3 in (1.30 m)
- Length: 16 ft 11 in (5.16 m)
- Height: 4 ft 9 in (1.45 m)

### Fuel

- Total Capacity: 406.8 US gal (2,736.5 lb / 1,541.0 L)
- Usable Fuel: 402.0 US gal (2,703.6 lb / 1,522.0 L)
- Unusable Fuel: 4.8 US gal (32.9 lb / 19.0 L)
- Approved Fuels: JET A, JET A-1, JP-5, JP-8, F-35, diesel (restricted operations)
- Anti-Icing Additive: NOT required (approved but not mandatory)

### Oil

- Total Capacity: 3.6 US gal
- Drain/Refill: 2.0 US gal
- Operating Range: 1.0 US gal
- Specification: MIL-PRF-23699 Type II

### Electrical

- Generator 1: 28 VDC, 300A continuous / 450A (2-min burst)
- Starter/Generator 2: 28 VDC, 300A continuous / 450A (2-min burst)
- Battery: 24V, 44 Ah (Li-Ion, MSN 3001+) or 42 Ah

### Avionics

- Honeywell Primus Epic 2.0 avionics suite
- 4 display units (primary and multifunction)
- Modular Avionics Units (MAU) with AIOP modules
- Advanced Graphics Modules (AGM)
- Multi-Function Controller (MFC) and Touch Screen Controller (TSC)
- Dual FMS, dual AHRS, dual ADC
- TAWS Class A or B (configuration dependent)
- TCAS I or II (configuration dependent)
- ADS-B Out and In (MSN 1720, 2001+)
- Synthetic Vision System (optional)
