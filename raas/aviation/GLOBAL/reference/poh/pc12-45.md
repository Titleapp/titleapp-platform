## Section 1 -- General

### Aircraft Description

Single-engine turboprop, pressurized, retractable tricycle landing gear.
Manufacturer: Pilatus Aircraft Ltd., Stans, Switzerland.
MSN Applicability: 101-544, 546-888

### Engine

- Type: Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6A-67B
- Configuration: Free turbine, reverse-flow annular combustion chamber
- FADEC: None (conventional fuel control)
- Maximum Shaft Horsepower: 1,200 SHP (takeoff, 5 min limit)
- Maximum Continuous / Climb / Cruise SHP: 1,000 SHP
- Ng Speed at 104%: 38,100 rpm
- Np (Propeller Speed): 1,700 rpm
- Propeller Low Speed: 1,550 rpm

### Propeller

- Type: Hartzell HC-E4A-3D/E10477K or HC-E4A-3D/E10477SK
- Blades: 4-blade, constant speed, full feathering, reversible
- Diameter: 104-105 in (2.642-2.667 m)
- Fine Pitch: 19 deg
- Reverse Pitch: -17.5 deg
- Feather Pitch: 79.6 deg
- Minimum In-Flight Pitch: 6 deg

### Dimensions

- Wingspan: 53 ft 4 in (16.28 m)
- Length: 47 ft 3 in (14.40 m)
- Height: 14 ft 0 in (4.26 m)
- Wing Area: 277.8 sq ft (25.81 sq m)

### Cabin Dimensions

- Width: 5 ft 0 in (1.52 m)
- Floor Width: 4 ft 3 in (1.30 m)
- Length: 16 ft 11 in (5.16 m)
- Height: 4 ft 9 in (1.45 m)

### Fuel

- Total Capacity: 402.0 US gal (2,704 lb / 1,522 L)
- Usable Fuel: 396.0 US gal (2,663 lb / 1,499 L)
- Unusable Fuel: 6.0 US gal (41 lb / 23 L)
- Approved Fuels: JET A, JET A-1, JP-5, JP-8
- Anti-Icing Additive: REQUIRED below 0 deg C (MIL-DTL-27686 or MIL-DTL-85470, 0.06-0.15% concentration)

### Oil

- Total Capacity: 3.6 US gal
- Drain/Refill: 2.0 US gal
- Operating Range: 1.0 US gal
- Specification: MIL-PRF-23699 Type II

### Electrical

- Starter/Generator: 28 VDC, 300A continuous / 450A (2-min burst)
- Second Generator: 28 VDC, 115A continuous only (no burst rating)
- Battery: 24V, 42 Ah (NiCd)

### Key Differences from NG and NGX

- Engine: PT6A-67B vs PT6A-67P (NG) vs PT6E-67XP (NGX)
- No FADEC on any PT6A variant; NGX has FADEC
- Lower ITT limits: 800 deg C takeoff (vs 850 NG/NGX), 760 deg C cruise (vs 820 NG/NGX)
- Lower max takeoff/ramp weight: 9,921/9,965 lb (vs 10,450/10,495 NG/NGX)
- Landing weight equals takeoff weight (9,921 lb) -- no separate reduction
- VMO: 236 KIAS (vs 240 NG/NGX)
- VLE: 236 KIAS (vs 240 NGX)
- Second generator: 115A only, no burst rating (vs 300A/450A on NG/NGX)
- Anti-icing additive: REQUIRED below 0 deg C (not required on NG/NGX)
- Conventional avionics (varies by MSN: Honeywell Primus 1000, BendixKing, or APEX retrofit)
