# Pilatus PC-12 NG -- Pilot's Information Manual Reference

Document: PC-12 NG PIM, Report 02277, PC-12/47E, Rev 14
Aircraft Model: PC-12/47E (NG variant)
EASA Type Certificate: EASA.A.123

---

## Section 1 -- General

### Aircraft Description

Single-engine turboprop, pressurized, retractable tricycle landing gear.
Manufacturer: Pilatus Aircraft Ltd., Stans, Switzerland.
MSN Applicability: 545, 1001-1719

### Engine

- Type: Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6A-67P
- Configuration: Free turbine, reverse-flow annular combustion chamber
- FADEC: None (conventional fuel control)
- Maximum Shaft Horsepower: 1,200 SHP (takeoff and max climb)
- Maximum Cruise SHP: 1,000 SHP
- Ng Speed at 104%: 38,100 rpm
- Np (Propeller Speed): 1,700 rpm
- Propeller Low Speed: 1,550 rpm

### Propeller

- Type: Hartzell HC-E4A-3D/E10477SK
- Blades: 4-blade, constant speed, full feathering, reversible
- Diameter: 104-105 in (2.642-2.667 m)
- Fine Pitch: 19 deg
- Reverse Pitch: -17.5 deg
- Feather Pitch: 79.6 deg
- Minimum In-Flight Pitch: 6 deg

### Dimensions

- Wingspan: 53 ft 4 in (16.28 m)
- Length: 47 ft 3 in (14.40 m)
- Height: 14 ft 0 in (4.26 m)
- Wing Area: 277.8 sq ft (25.81 sq m)

### Cabin Dimensions

- Width: 5 ft 0 in (1.52 m)
- Floor Width: 4 ft 3 in (1.30 m)
- Length: 16 ft 11 in (5.16 m)
- Height: 4 ft 9 in (1.45 m)

### Fuel

- Total Capacity: 402.0 US gal (2,704 lb / 1,522 L)
- Usable Fuel: 396.0 US gal (2,663 lb / 1,499 L)
- Unusable Fuel: 6.0 US gal (41 lb / 23 L)
- Approved Fuels: JET A, JET A-1, JP-5, JP-8
- Anti-Icing Additive: NOT required (approved but not mandatory)

### Oil

- Total Capacity: 3.6 US gal
- Drain/Refill: 2.0 US gal
- Operating Range: 1.0 US gal
- Specification: MIL-PRF-23699 Type II

### Electrical

- Generator 1: 28 VDC, 300A continuous / 450A (2-min burst)
- Starter/Generator 2: 28 VDC, 300A continuous / 450A (2-min burst)
- Battery: 24V, 42 Ah (NiCd)

### Avionics

- Honeywell Primus APEX avionics suite
- 4 display units (primary and multifunction)
- Dual AHRS, dual ADC
- Dual FMS (Honeywell)
- TAWS Class B (standard) or Class A (optional)
- TCAS I or II (optional)
- Weather radar (Honeywell Primus 660)
- ADS-B Out

### Key Differences from NGX

- Engine: PT6A-67P (conventional fuel control, no FADEC) vs PT6E-67XP (FADEC)
- Propeller: 4-blade vs 5-blade
- Cruise SHP: 1,000 vs 1,100
- MMO: 0.48 vs 0.49
- No Touch Screen Controller (TSC) or Cursor Control Device (CCD)
- No ADS-B In standard
- No Synthetic Vision System standard
