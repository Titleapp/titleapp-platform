## Document Structure

The FSI PC12NG Materials PDF is a comprehensive training compilation containing
five major sections totaling 4,209 pages:

### Part 1 — FSI Safety Briefs (Pages 1-30)
- Runway Excursion Prevention (Rev 0.0)
  - Statistics: 41% of business jet accidents (2017-2022), 79% on landing
  - Mitigation: precision delivery, stable approach criteria, go-around mindset
  - Threat/error management for takeoff and landing excursions
- GNSS Interference Pilot Guide (Rev 0.0)
  - Jamming vs Spoofing — definitions and mechanisms
  - 41,000 flights reported interference in one month (Jul-Aug 2024)
  - Affected systems: FMS, guidance, hybrid IRS, clock, GPWS, Wx Radar, CPDLC, ADS-B
  - Mitigation procedures: pre-flight, in-area, post-spoofing, after landing
  - SBAS review: WAAS, EGNOS, SDCM, MSAS/QZSS, GAGAN

  - V-speeds configuration and modification

- Weather Radar (Wx Radar)
  - Modes, tilt management, gain
  - Turbulence detection
  - Ground mapping
