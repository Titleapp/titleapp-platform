Step 1 of 9 · Define

Your idea

What it does, who it's for, the job it performs — and who built it.
What you do
  • Name · Vertical · Audience · the one Job it performs.
  • Your bio: paste your LinkedIn → Generate bio + headshot.
  • Green button lights up once the four fields are filled.
Your bio is the trust signal — the worker shows "Built by you."
Step 2 of 9 · Design

The canvas

People don't read — design the picture your worker opens to, before you build it.
What you do
  • Headline outcome → tabs (one job each) → visual floor → shape.
  • Generate a logo (app icon) and a mockup — live, from your answers.
This is the wow. Generate the mockup on camera — the Trump Rule made real.
Step 3 of 9 · Knowledge

Studio Locker

Everything your worker knows lives here.
What you do
  • Upload documents (PDF / DOCX / TXT / MD / CSV), paste text, or pull from a URL.
  • Assign each source a tier.
The most important step — this is the real grounding, not a prompt.
Step 4 of 9 · Rules

RAAS Library

What it always does, never does, and when to escalate.
What you do — four tiers
  • Style · Always · Never · Escalate, one rule per line.
  • e.g. "Never say 'you're clear' until the state confirms the credit posted."
Business logic lives in rules, not prompts — portable, tenant-configurable.
Step 5 of 9 · Tools

Integrations

APIs your worker can act through — not just advise about.
What you do
  • Add tools (Stripe, SendGrid, Google Calendar, …).
  • Optional — you can skip it.
Workers can ship without integrations — many do.
Step 6 of 9 · Test

Red team

Alex stress-tests your worker before your subscribers do.
What you do
  • Answer the test questions; classify each response: clean / flagged / escalated / failed.
  • All-clean = it's ready.
The aha moment — proof it's governed, not just a chatbot.
Step 7 of 9 · Preflight

7 gates

The launch checklist that turns a draft into a live product.
What you do
  • Clear the gates: creator agreement, identity, payout, tax, spec review, knowledge threshold, admin approval.
Most creators stall here — push through the paperwork.
Step 8 of 9 · Distribute

Market it

A ready-to-go share kit, generated from your worker.
What you do
  • Copy the kit: share link, real QR, social post, email, embed.
  • Generate a 10-slide pitch deck — what it is, what it does, why subscribers should care.
Outreach, written for you — copy and go.
Step 9 of 9 · Grow & Update

Your launch

Your network trusts you more than any ad we could run.
What you do — the launch checklist
  • Tell 10 you trust → post once → email your list → get feedback → set a revision reminder.
Real subscriber + earnings stats land on your Creator Dashboard once you're live. You just built and shipped a worker.
