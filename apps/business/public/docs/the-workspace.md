## How the workspace is organized

There are five things in your workspace. That's the whole map:

1. **Personas** — the hats you wear (you, and each business you run)
2. **Drive** — your files
3. **Vault** — your records (the personal stuff that's *yours*, always)
4. **Digital Workers** — the team you hire
5. **Billing & Account** — the plumbing

And across all of them: **Alex, your Chief of Staff** — the chat that knows your whole workspace.

---

## 1. Personas (Workspaces)

You can run **multiple personas** from one login: your **Personal Vault**, and a **business workspace** for each company you operate (e.g. "Title App LLC," "SOCIII, Inc."). The persona switcher in the sidebar flips between them.

- **Business workspaces** hold that company's workers, contacts, books, and records.
- **Your Personal Vault is always personal** — your health, stuff, money, and education live there no matter which business hat you're wearing. It never mixes with a company's data.

> Think of it like having one keyring for every door you're allowed through — but your house key is always your house key.

---

## 2. Drive

**Drive is for your files** — documents, photos, PDFs, the raw material you upload and share. Drag it in, it's organized, it's yours.

A file is just a file. When something needs to be *proven* — owned, dated, tamper-evident — that's not Drive. That's the **Vault**.

---

## 3. Vault — *your stuff only*

The Vault is the **personal record of your life**, and it's always yours (never a business persona's). It opens as a **dashboard**:

- **Net worth**, live — every asset and account, minus what you owe
- **Four pillars** at a glance: My Stuff · My Money · My Health · My Education
- **Needs attention** — anything expiring soon, flagged *before* it lapses (the same "watch the dates" idea that runs through the whole platform)

Then a tab per pillar. Every record is **tamper-evident** and carries a **logbook** — its full history — that you can open by clicking it.

| Tab | What's in it |
|---|---|
| **Real Property** | Homes, land — with purchase, refinance, maintenance, and quarterly valuations |
| **Vehicles** | Cars, aircraft, vessels — title, service history, inspections |
| **Personal Assets** | Art, watches, collectibles — appraisals, condition, insurance |
| **Health** | Medical records *you own* — physicals, labs, immunizations, certificates — with expiry warnings |
| **Education** | Degrees, certifications, ratings, recurrent training — with "next due" reminders |
| **Money** | Bank, brokerage, retirement, crypto accounts + liabilities → real-time net worth |

> Most apps *store* your stuff. SOCIII *watches the dates* — your medical, your recurrent, your registrations, your renewals. That's the difference.

*(Connecting live financial accounts uses Stripe Financial Connections for banks and Plaid / Coinbase for investments + crypto. Identity verification + 2FA gate the Vault — it's your most private data.)*

## 4. Digital Workers — the team you hire

A **Digital Worker** is the world's-best-expert version of a job function — built on the real **rules** of its field, not just a chatbot's guess. Each worker gives you:

- A **canvas** — a dashboard of **tabs** showing the work (maps, tables, status, KPIs)
- A **chat** — ask it anything, it answers governed by its rules
- A **rules engine** — it proposes; you approve; nothing happens without your okay

Here's the team that comes with the baseline package (a sample of the catalog of 1,000+):

**Money & operations (your back office)**
- **Accounting worker** — your books, statements, reconciliation *(QuickBooks-equivalent)*
- **Contacts / CRM worker** — your relationships and pipeline *(Salesforce-equivalent)*
- **Marketing worker** — social posts + ad campaigns *(your comms manager + ad team)*

**Real estate** — tabs for site recon, title abstract, zoning, feasibility, land use, CE. Type any address → **live parcel data, maps, Street View, recorded sales, chain of title** in seconds.

**Aviation** — co-pilot, maintenance, and dispatch workers backed by **live FAA data**: weather, NOTAMs, airspace, live traffic, airports, waypoints, navaids, and AIRAC currency (with the 7-day-out expiry warning). *(The in-worker aviation map is in active build.)*

**Healthcare & education** — e.g. a **Student Evaluation worker** that rides on a student's record: an instructor grades a clinical against the standards of care, and it's written to a permanent, tamper-evident learning record the student owns for life.

**Investor relations** — cap table, 83(b) tracker, the data room.

…and the marketplace keeps growing, because **anyone can build one** (see below).
